Matvey Vasilyev
Educational long-read on theory and history of linguistics and how it influenced each one of us. It considers how the systems of thinking formed under the influence of linguistic factors, which contain historical, political, religious, social, and other characteristics, are reflected in an individual person or even in a whole culture. These linguistic factors influence the worldview of speakers of a particular language, leaving a mark on how people perceive certain phenomena and things.
