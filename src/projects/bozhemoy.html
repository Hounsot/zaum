About project
2021 – 2022 — Educational project
A festival where sinful souls get a second chance to try and earn a spot in the gods' paradise among many others. Once a year, the festival is organized and through a questionnaire, souls deemed worthy of a second chance are selected. During the festival, they must convince the gods to take them to their paradise.
