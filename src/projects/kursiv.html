About project
2022 – 2023 — Educational team project
“Kursiv” is a media outlet that tells about typography and Russian designers. A design system was created that includes unified rules for formatting textual and graphical elements, color schemes, and typography. This helped to create a coordinated and recognizable visual style. The aim is to provide a quality system with information on Cyrillic fonts even for novice designers.
